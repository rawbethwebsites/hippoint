Est. 2020 · Our Story

For Work
& Play.

We are Hippoint — a Lagos-born brand built on the belief that African fashion belongs everywhere: in the office, on the weekend, at the dinner table, on the dance floor.

🌺

The Vision

Built on African Essentials

Hippoint was born from a simple frustration: why couldn't African prints be the default — not the occasion? In 2020, our founder looked at a wardrobe full of beautiful Ankara pieces she only brought out for events, and decided to change everything.

We redesigned African textiles for the everyday woman and man — the ones who move between worlds, who hold meetings and make memories, who don't need a reason to look extraordinary.

Every piece in our collection starts with a fabric sourced from artisan weavers across West Africa. We work directly with our makers, ensuring fair wages, sustainable practices, and the preservation of weaving traditions that deserve to be seen by the world.

— Hippoint, est. Lagos 2020 🌹

What We Stand For

The Four Pillars of
Hippoint

🌿

Comfort

Clothes that feel as good as they look. We use breathable, quality fabrics that keep up with Lagos heat and London winters alike.

🌍

Culture

Every print tells a story. We work with artisans to honour traditions — not reduce them to trends. Wear your heritage, carry your roots.

✨

Quality

We produce fewer pieces, better made. Each garment is checked twice before it leaves our hands. No compromises on construction or craft.

☀️

Everyday Wear

African basics aren't for special occasions. They're for Mondays. For Fridays. For the school run and the board meeting and the Sunday market.
